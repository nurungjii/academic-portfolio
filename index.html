David Park

Hello! I am an undergraduate researcher studying computer science and mathematics at Washington University in St. Louis. I am currently affiliated with Washu's NLP and MiG Group where I research and develop novel ideas in natural language processing and deep reinforcement learning. My main areas of interests lie in the scaling and interpretability of large language models, and cognitive language models that exhibit human-like reasoning capabilities and generalization. Currently, I am working on optimizing decentralized multi-agent reinforcement learning algorithms through improved code compilation and search algorithms. Outside of academia, I enjoy movies, books, and cooking.
