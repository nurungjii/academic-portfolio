David Park

Hello! I am an undergraduate researcher studying computer science and mathematics at Washington University in St. Louis. I am currently affiliated with Washu's NLP and MiG Group where I research and develop novel ideas in natural language processing and deep reinforcement learning. My main areas of interest lie in developing cognitive language models that can exhibit human-like generalization and emotional intelligence. Currently, I'm investigating the scaling capabilities of current LLMs as well as models for multi-agent reinforcement learning. Outside of academia, I like to watch movies, read books, and cook.
